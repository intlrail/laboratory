ATTRACTORS IN DYNAMICAL SYSTEMS

Phase space trajectories of several notable dynamical systems (Lorenz, Rössler, Thomas, Chua, Lü-Chen, Sprott-Linz, and Rabinovich-Fabrikant, in order of presentation). Trajectories are integrated with a sympletic Euler scheme. Written with javascript/WebGL.

  • LMB/WHEEL: move camera
  • LEFT/RIGHT/SPACE: previous/next/reset system
  • 1/Q,2/W,3/E: adjust system parameters

J. Lo, March 2017.
