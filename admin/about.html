    International Rail, officially registered as the International Railroad Corporation and commonly shortened as IR, is the world's largest transportation network and postal service connecting 52 hubs around the seven continents and 12 colonial stations across the four terrestrial planets in the Solar System.

    International Rail Enterprises

    International Rail Enterprises is a multinational conglomerate incorporated in the city-state of Singapore and headquartered in Bogota, Colombia. Originally founded in 2112 as a joint venture by the 11 member states of the Equatorial Union (EQU), the core mission of International Rail is to support the policies of the Union by facilitating multilateral cooperation in various areas of mutual interest, including economic activities, scientific research and technological development.

    In accordance with the EQU Charter, International Rail currently operates across several different segments within the global economy. Historically, the company's four most well-known services consist of genetic engineering, pharmaceutical manufacturing, renewable energy production and mass transportation.
